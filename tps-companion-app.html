"Give me an example of a time you utilized followership to obtain a positive outcome — in the station or on an incident."
1:30
Target response time
STAR FORMAT
Sit → Task → Action → Result
Coach's Note
Force yourself to say "What I chose to do was..." before your action step. That decision language — why that, not something else — is what assessors score most heavily in Layer 2.
Common Pitfall
Stopping at the outcome ("it worked out well") without naming the specific decision that produced the outcome. Assessors mark this as a 2, not a 4.
